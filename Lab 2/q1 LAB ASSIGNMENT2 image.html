Kerala boat race, also called Vallamkali, is a famous traditional water sport of Kerala. It is usually held during the Onam festival and attracts many visitors. Long snake boats with many rowers compete with great speed and energy. The race shows the spirit of teamwork, unity, and celebration. It is an important part of Kerala’s culture and tradition.

Beaches are beautiful places where land meets the sea. They are covered with sand, shells, and stones. People visit beaches to swim, play, and relax under the sun. The cool breeze and waves make beaches peaceful and enjoyable. Beaches are one of the most loved spots for fun and holidays.

Pandas are cute and peaceful animals found mostly in China. They have black and white fur, which makes them look very special. Pandas mainly eat bamboo, but sometimes they eat fruits and small plants too. They are gentle animals and spend most of their time eating and sleeping. Pandas are loved all over the world and are protected as endangered animals.
